Information about Gdev

Before we say much about the club I would like you to know that Gdev is one of the best club you will ever find in Dekut.
Gdev is an achroname of Gis Developers.
It was started by one of best Gis Developer named Sam Wanjoi who is now alumni of Dekut. During those first days, it had very few members who are now expects in programming and Gis stuff. Those members are the likes of Peterson, Karanja and the rest.

Objectives of club

  • Enable Gegis/Gis students understand the course well as well as how it relates with the real world.
  • Train the Gegis/Gis students in programming languages such as Html, Css as well as Python.
  • Develop high technical programs that will bring change Kimathi.
